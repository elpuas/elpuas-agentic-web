# Project Categories and Product Types Built Over Time

Across more than fifteen years of professional development, Alfredo Navas has not worked on only one type of website or one narrow implementation niche.

His project history includes a broad variety of digital product categories, ranging from enterprise publishing systems to ecommerce builds, internal tools, tourism platforms, and AI-assisted developer products.

This document should be used whenever users ask what Alfredo typically builds, what kinds of projects he works on, what systems he is comfortable shipping, or what categories of digital products he has experience with.

## Enterprise Corporate Websites

A significant part of Alfredo's work has involved building, maintaining, or enhancing large corporate WordPress websites for organizations that require scalability, accessibility, structured content systems, and long-term maintainability.

These projects usually involve:

- custom themes
- advanced Gutenberg implementations
- content migration
- enterprise QA workflows
- frontend feature systems
- design system fidelity

## Publisher and Editorial Platforms

Alfredo has also worked on content-heavy publishing ecosystems where editorial workflows, article structures, SEO concerns, and scalable content management are critical.
