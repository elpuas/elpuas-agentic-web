## Background

I started as a visual artist before becoming a developer.

- Early interest in drawing, comics, and illustration
- Created original comic work, including a local comic project in Costa Rica
- Explored street art, graffiti, and tattoo design

---

## Transition to Digital

- Studied and worked in graphic design
- Created brochures, ads, and print materials for local brands
- Gained experience with Adobe tools and visual design systems

---

## Move to Web Development

- Transitioned from design to web development during the early web era
- Learned HTML, CSS, PHP, and JavaScript
- Combined design skills with development to build better user experiences

---

## Career Growth

- Worked with agencies, startups, and distributed teams
- Specialized in frontend development and WordPress
- Progressed into a Senior Frontend Engineer role

---

## Creative Identity

Even today, I still approach development as a creative discipline.

- Background in art influences UI/UX decisions
- Focus on clarity, visual structure, and usability
- Continue exploring creative projects outside of development

---

## Summary

My work is shaped by a combination of:

- Art and design
- Technical development
- Real-world problem solving

This mix allows me to build products that are both functional and well-designed.
